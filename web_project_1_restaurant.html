Our Story

Authentic Flavors Since 1995

Founded by Chef Ramesh Kumar, Spice Garden has been serving authentic Indian cuisine for over 25 years. Our recipes are passed down through generations, using the finest ingredients sourced directly from farms.

Every dish tells a story of tradition, culture, and the love of cooking. Come experience the magic of real Indian flavors.
